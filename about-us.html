ABOUT SATWIK

Satwik Sanstha is a national, non-for-profit organization and deeply engaged in social, cultural and human welfare activities. Satwik Sanstha is registered in India under the Societies Registration Act of 21,1860 with its registered office at Pryagraj. Registration number 431/2004, 2005 on the 15/07/2004. It is an organization which is run by a group of well-motivated youth who work in the areas of Slums people, Disabled children, Old age home people and child education for the underprivileged and societal awareness.
